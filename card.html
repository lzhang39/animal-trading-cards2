Pomsky

The Teacup Pomsky is simply the smallest variety of this dog that is available, weighing less than 7 lbs.

  • Origin: United States
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Average Weight: 30 pounds
  • Mix of: Husky and Pomeranian

A unique designer dog whose look will be a mix of both breeds, creating the small and cuddly Pomsky.
